# Product Roadmap

## Vision

UnCollateral aims to become the leading undercollateralized lending protocol in DeFi by leveraging verifiable reputation data to enable capital-efficient borrowing.

## Feature Backlog

### High Priority

1. **Emergency Pause Mechanism**
   - Owner can pause in emergencies
   - Time-lock for unpause
   - Estimated: 1 week

2. **Price Oracle Integration**
   - Chainlink price feeds
   - Dynamic collateral tracking
   - Estimated: 2 weeks

3. **Improved Interest Model**
   - Utilization-based rates
   - Borrower reputation factor
   - Estimated: 1 week

4. **NFT Receipt Tokens**
   - For lender deposits
   - Tradeable positions
   - Estimated: 2 weeks

### Medium Priority

5. **Credit Delegation**
   - Users can delegate their credit line
   - Split reputation benefits
   - Estimated: 3 weeks

6. **Partial Repayments**
   - Pay down loan gradually
   - Proportional collateral release
   - Estimated: 1 week

7. **Loan Extensions**
   - Extend loan duration
   - Additional interest
   - Estimated: 1 week

8. **Referral Program**
   - Earn rewards for referring users
   - Bonus for both parties
   - Estimated: 2 weeks

### Low Priority

9. **Social Recovery**
   - Recover account via social attestations
   - Estimated: 3 weeks

10. **Reputation Staking**
    - Stake reputation for better terms
    - Slashing for defaults
    - Estimated: 4 weeks
